](http://mp.weixin.qq.com/s?biz=MzkwMTQwMzExNQ==&mid=2247484351&idx=1&sn=66a9ae22d29b1970e3ae4f3cbcda9e6f&chksm=c0b41b3bf7c3922d83cfad1513eebbc050bcc3997e562bc9c862054a2a0398d6af82351c2b74&scene=21#wechat_redirect)

小朋友没有“[合一
](http://mp.weixin.qq.com/s?biz=MzkwMTQwMzExNQ==&mid=2247484401&idx=1&sn=f1db0b5780dfd04d59ddfa4f4f81b20b&chksm=c0b41b75f7c392635c42de76d65cb4d653e649cc865b6a0721bfa2de8e424831930b8132fc05&scene=21#wechat_redirect)

[控制意识
](http://mp.weixin.qq.com/s?biz=MzkwMTQwMzExNQ==&mid=2247484460&idx=1&sn=71a133bf0aeef204767618f8c6aba24b&chksm=c0b41ca8f7c395be4e4288816b69faa8794353654202fcc228aa9392bf9bc9ac57059697d4f2&scene=21#wechat_redirect)

小朋友常常以自由意识为主导，更容易提升灵性，而大多数成年人是以控制意识为主导，更容易陷入[矩阵
](http://mp.weixin.qq.com/s?biz=MzAxMzg5MDI2MA==&mid=2247491513&idx=1&sn=abb2ca2b591432b7d4c530cdcc26723a&chksm=9b9af802aced71144be4f84d4724043f6752990a70bd5debaa58dbee8bcdfb2266267052508e&scene=21#wechat_redirect)
而不自知。看来我们要多向小朋友学习，学习他们的灵动和心态，因为觉醒是把控制意识转换成自由意识，借此脱离矩阵。
